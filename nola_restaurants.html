Welcome to the Official New Orleans restaurant guide.

This site is about Carissa's 5 favorite restaurants in New Orleans, Louisiana.

Here they are:

  1. Blue Oak BBQ

    Best Mac and Cheese in the heart of Mid-City!

  2. 1000 Figs

    Great for brunch with friends, or eat all the pita by yourself.

  3. 14 Parishes

    Black-owned Jamaican soul food joint, and boy is there soul in these flavors!

  4. Breads on Oak

    Vegan sandwiches, pastries, and BREAD. Try the Muffanada if you find your way here.

  5. Velvet Cactus

    The vibes are immaculate at this Tex-Mex oasis in Lakeview. The Southwest Eggrolls with Raspberry dipping sauce? No words can describe..

  6. A Secret 6th Location: My Kitchen
